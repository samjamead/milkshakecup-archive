Welcome from the Defending Champion

The Milkshake Cup is the most prestigious event in the amateur golfing calendar. Strictly invite only, you should feel honoured to have been selected for this year's event. Winning the Milkshake Cup is one of my greatest achievements; lifting that famous trophy and being awarded the green waterproof jacket in McDonald's car park is truly a feeling like no other.

Short drives, aggressive chipping, and slow bobbling putts are the key to success on these challenging courses. May the odds be ever in your favour!

T. R. Clements
– 2018 Champion

Date and Time

11am, Sunday 15th September 2019

Location

Westcountry Golf Academy, Tiverton Parkway, EX16 7EH

Format

Handicapped strokeplay over 18 holes (2 x 9 hole rounds combined).

We will play nine holes, stop for a bite to eat, then play the second nine holes. Second round groupings will be determined by the score after nine holes. The winner is the golfer with the lowest net score after 18 holes!

For golfers, 1/2 handicap appies. For non golfers, handicap will be set at 36, therefore handicap of 18 on the day.
